Hi! I'm Siddhant, a PhD student studying astrophysics
at the University of Heidelberg.

I work in the Galactic Archaeology group at the Landessternwarte (LSW) (State Observatory),
and investigate non-equilibrium chemical processes in stellar atmospheres.
My main research interests are stellar atmospheres, exoplanets and machine learning
